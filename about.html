Hello! I’m a college student majoring in Computer Science with a growing love for technology, building things that work (and occasionally crash), and solving puzzles with code. I'm currently learning the fundamentals of programming, web development, and electronics—and having a blast doing it.

Whether it's debugging a stubborn piece of C++ code or getting an LED to blink on an Arduino board, I find joy in the little wins and lessons along the way. Every bug is a mini-mystery waiting to be cracked.

When I’m not buried in code, you might find me collaborating in student coding clubs, volunteering at campus events, or watching videos on how the internet actually works. I believe in learning by doing, failing forward, and always staying curious.

My long-term goal? To become a developer who builds tools that empower others—and to never stop learning.
